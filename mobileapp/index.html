Tap the Exchange section in the bottom control menu of the main screen and select the Buy tab.
On Changelly, you can buy cryptocurrencies with your local fiat currency in a couple of steps:

  • Select the cryptocurrency you would like to buy, the fiat currency you want to purchase crypto with, and your country of residence.
  • Look through all the available rates suggested by our partners. Choose the one that suits you best.
  • Enter your destination address (crypto wallet address), and dont forget to fill in a Memo or a Destination Tag if it is required. You will be redirected to the providers page, where you should go through the verification process.
  • Check all the details and enter your payment information. Estimated transaction time depends on the provider, your bank, and your chosen payment method.
  • Thats it! Your new crypto will be in your wallet very soon.

Tap the Exchange section in the bottom control menu of the main screen and select the Exchange tab.

  • Select the cryptocurrency pair you would like to exchange. Then choose the exchange rate from the two available options: fixed or floating. According to the selected rate, you will see the approximate (if floating) or exact (if fixed) amount that you will receive.
    Double-check the details and tap the Exchange button.
  • If you have selected a floating rate, enter your destination address (the crypto wallet address) where you plan to receive your funds. You can copy and paste addresses or use the QR scanner within the Changelly app.
    Transaction details like the exchange rate, network and exchange fees, and the approximate amount that you will receive will be displayed.
    If you have selected a fixed rate, enter both your destination and refund addresses. You can copy and paste addresses or use the QR scanner within the Changelly app. Transaction details like the amount you’re sending, the exchange rate, and the exact amount you will receive will be displayed.
    If everything is correct, tap the “Next step” button.
  • Follow the instructions on the screen. Send the indicated amount to the Changelly address within the time period you see on the timer.
    That’s it! The exchanged cryptocurrency will arrive in your crypto wallet within a few minutes.
